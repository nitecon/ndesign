Asides

Edge-pinned callouts that fold onto the page — outer corners straight, inner corners rounded.

Right Aside (Default)

This aside pins to the right edge of the viewport, with its inner corners rounded and outer corners flush against the page edge.

Left Aside

Same concept mirrored — pins to the left edge. The rounded corners always face inward toward the content.

Content flows normally between asides. The fold effect gives them visual weight without disrupting the layout.

Aside Inside a Column

When used inside a grid column, the aside still bleeds to the right edge.
